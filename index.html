Our Legacy

The Legend of Haveli

Nestled in the heart of Patna's vibrant Phulwarisharif area, Haveli Restaurant is a premium dining destination designed to look like a modern palace. Our signature interior boasts beautiful glass features, glowing golden frames, and soft booth seating ideal for families, couples, and grand parties.

We pride ourselves on sourcing the freshest ingredients and grinding our masalas in-house to capture the authentic, traditional flavor of local Indian cuisine. Whether it's our signature Haveli Special Chicken, butter paneer masala, or fresh tandoori kulchas, every dish is crafted to perfection.
